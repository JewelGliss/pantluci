as much as possible. By mid June 2022 the now sole particle used
in the language was extremely repetitive and that became became
a larger and larger problem forcing a redo of the morphology and
phonology in order to allow for reduplication and a greater focus
on aesthetics. By July 2022, the language expanded into a research
project exploring esoteric languages in general, with the language
used as an example. Its phonology was once again scrapped and
rebuilt with a focus on lling the same purpose as the grammar. By

8.2.3 Morphology
Pantluci’s syllables may be analyzed as having a form anywhere from
(C)V(m), (an optional consonant followed by a vowel or syllabic conso-
nant followed by an optional nasal coda). /p/, /t/, and /k/ may act
as though they were syllables during word construction. The sequences
/aa/, /pp/, /ttC/ (where C is a non-syllabic consonant) , and /kk/ do not
occur. Syllables with both syllabic consonants and codas do not occur.
No words end with /p/, /t/, or /k/. Ambiguous null onsets (always root
initial) are marked with �h�. Stress rests on the rst syllable containing a
vowel. Words must contain at least one vowel. Syllabic consonants may
not occur anywhere in a word before the stressed vowel.
Content words in Pantluci undergo a morphological process in order
to denote the grammatical structure of the sentence. This process consists
of replacing the rst vowel sequence with /a/ if there are 0 preceeding
nodes, /i/ if there is 1, then as the number increases /u/, /ai/, /au/,
/aia/, /aua/, etc.
pantluci → pintluci → puntluci → paintluci → ...
Interjections have any other intial vowel sequence: /ia/, /iu/, /ua/, /ui/,
etc.

8.3.5 Ad-phrases
Prepositions, Adverbs, and ad-phrases modify the fundamental meanings

possibilities you meant, they instead alter the meaning of the words

specifying details about the house as “red house” does. This does not
mean that it always fundamentally changes the meaning as “fake” does;
some prepositions nearly add small semantic changes like “to,” “at,” or
“from” do in English.

8.4 Compounding
8.4.1 Joining
The actual morphological process for compounding has a few stages
depending on how useful and common the semantics of the compound
end up being.
1. loaning (incl self loaning) loans can have letters added or removed
based on commonness apaplca => apaca
2. The most basic form of compounding is to take two or more roots,
then for each of the roots other than the rst one, if the rst
vowel has a nasal coda remove the vowel, otherwise replace the
vowel with an “l”. This turns them into syllabic consonants due
their position. Then attach all of the roots together in order. If
any of the syllabic “n”s or “l”s would cause phonemes from the
